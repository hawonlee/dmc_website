Welcome to the CMC!

BookIt is the interface for checking out equipment from the CMC. We have a wide range of equipment including cameras, microphones, and more. You can also check out in-lab resources like computers, software, and other tools for your projects!

Before you can check-out items, you must first become a member of the CMC. Follow these steps to become a member:

1

Ensure your Eligibility

Membership is free for students who are full-time AND a part of KSAS/WSE/Peabody. All other students pay a one-time $50 fee. For more details, see our FAQ or Contact Us.

2

Come to the CMC

Come in person during our open hours. We are at 3003 N Charles St, right between FedEx and Homewood Apartments, underneath The LaB.

3

Talk to our Staff

Let the front-desk staff know that it's your first time and that you'd like to become a member.

4

Watch a Video & Fill out a Form

We will show you a short video and have you fill out a short form online.

5

Check your Membership Below

After our staff enter you into our online system, you should be able to check your membership & authorizations here.
